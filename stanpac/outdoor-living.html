Welcome to Standard Pacific Homes! We know there are many decisions that go into purchasing a home and we are glad you have chosen to get to know more about our product and the quality of homes we build.

This website has been designed to assist you in your home buying decision making process. It shows many of the options available in construction and design that can help customize your home and make it uniquely yours. Patio

View Options

Transform your outdoor living space into a stunning backyard retreat by choosing an outdoor kitchen or outdoor fireplace.

These options will provide a stylish space to relax and entertain outdoors. The outdoor kitchen includes a grill, granite countertops with an option for a sink and faucet where you can prepare and serve food outdoors with ease and convenience.

The outdoor fireplace will provide warmth and ambiance on your patio on those chilly evenings while you enjoy your outdoor living room. Creating an outdoor living room will expand your options for entertaining and enjoying your dream home while taking advantage of the beautiful Texas climate.
