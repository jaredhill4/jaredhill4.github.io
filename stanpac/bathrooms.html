Carpet

Twist, density, color and style are important factors to consider when selecting a carpet that is right for your home. The twist of the carpet will affect the texture and look of cut pile carpet—the tighter the twist, the higher the performance rating. The higher density of the carpet affects the appearance of the carpet and provides greater comfort and luxury underfoot.

Carpet Pad

We have a ½" 6 pound or ½" 7 pound pad with moisture (vapor) barrier available for your selection. Choosing a higher density carpet pad can make your carpet last longer and feel better.

Wood

Hard wood floors offer many options no matter how diverse and unique your needs are. They will increase the value of your property and are a great long term investment. Available in various sizes, species and stains, hard wood floors give an elegant aesthetic and high end look.

Tile

The most durable of the flooring options offered, tile comes in many colors and textures. It can also be installed in various patterns to create the aesthetic you are going for. Patterns include straight, diagonal and brick joint.
