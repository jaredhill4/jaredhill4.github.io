Welcome to Standard Pacific Homes! We know there are many decisions that go into purchasing a home and we are glad you have chosen to get to know more about our product and the quality of homes we build.

This website has been designed to assist you in your home buying decision making process. It shows many of the options available in construction and design that can help customize your home and make it uniquely yours. It is a tool to guide you and help you prepare for your design appointment as well as give you more time to think about what features you would like include to specifically personalize your new home.

It is a comprehensive overlook at what is available throughout all of our communities. Pricing for upgraded options will be provided at your design appointment as prices frequently vary due to market fluctuations.

We hope you enjoy this website as you get started on creating your dream home!
